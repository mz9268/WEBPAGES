Espresso

Origin: Born in Italy in the early 20th century, espresso is the foundation of all coffee culture.

Description: A concentrated shot of pure coffee excellence. Our espresso is crafted from carefully selected Arabica beans, roasted to perfection in our mountain location. The result is a rich, intense flavor with a golden crema layer on top.

Ingredients:

  • Freshly ground Arabica coffee beans
  • Pure filtered mountain water

What Makes It Special: We use a traditional Italian brewing method with precise temperature control (195-205°F) and optimal extraction time (25-30 seconds) to achieve the perfect balance of sweetness, acidity, and body.

A strong, full-bodied coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. Served in a small cup, it's the perfect pick-me-up.

Price Range: 2.50 – 3.50 AED

Americano

Origin: Created during World War II when American soldiers diluted Italian espresso to match their familiar drip coffee.

Description: A smooth, clean coffee experience that highlights the natural flavors of our premium beans. Perfect for those who enjoy the strength of espresso with the volume of regular coffee.

Ingredients:

  • Double shot of our signature espresso
  • Hot filtered water
  • Optional: pure cow's milk or sugar

What Makes It Special: We maintain the perfect 1:2 ratio of espresso to water, preserving the coffee's complexity while creating a more approachable drink. Served at the ideal temperature for maximum flavor appreciation.

Latte

Origin: The Italian "caffè e latte" tradition, meaning "coffee and milk," dates back centuries.

Description:Silky smooth steamed milk perfectly combined with our rich espresso creates a creamy, comforting experience. Finished with delicate latte art that makes each cup a work of art.

Ingredients:

  • Single or double espresso shot
  • Pure local cow's milk (steamed to microfoam perfection)
  • Optional flavored syrups

What Makes It Special: We steam our pure cow's milk to exactly 150-160°F, creating microfoam that integrates seamlessly with the espresso. Our baristas craft unique latte art designs for every cup.

Cappuccino

Origin: Named after Capuchin monks in Italy, whose brown robes matched the drink's color.

Description: The perfect harmony of equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and thick foam. A classic that showcases the artisanal coffee-making tradition in its purest form.

Ingredients:

  • Single shot of espresso
  • Equal parts steamed pure cow's milk
  • Thick layer of milk foam
  • Optional: cocoa powder dusting

What Makes It Special: We follow traditional Italian proportions and use our signature pure cow's milk to create an exceptionally thick, velvety foam that holds its shape throughout your coffee experience.

Mocha

Origin: Named after the port city of Mocha in Yemen, where coffee beans were first exported to Europe.

Description: The perfect marriage of rich espresso and premium chocolate, topped with whipped cream. A decadent treat that satisfies both coffee and chocolate lovers.

Ingredients:

  • Double shot espresso
  • Premium chocolate syrup
  • Steamed pure cow's milk
  • Fresh whipped cream
  • Cocoa powder or chocolate shavings

What Makes It Special: We use authentic Belgian chocolate and create our own chocolate syrup in-house, ensuring a rich, complex flavor that complements rather than overpowers the coffee.

Frappe

Origin: Invented in Greece in 1957, the frappe became a symbol of Greek coffee culture.

Description: A refreshing iced coffee drink that's perfect for warm mountain afternoons. Creamy, sweet, and energizing with a delightful foam layer.

Ingredients:

  • Instant coffee or espresso shots
  • Cold water and ice
  • Sugar or sweetener
  • Pure cow's milk or cream
  • Optional: vanilla extract

What Makes It Special: We blend our ingredients at high speed to create the signature thick foam, then serve it ice-cold in tall glasses perfect for sipping while enjoying mountain views.

Blue Mojito

Origin: A modern twist on the classic Cuban mojito, incorporating natural blue coloring from butterfly pea flowers.

Description: A stunning azure-colored refresher that changes color when citrus is added. Fresh, minty, and visually spectacular.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Lime juice
  • Natural blue coloring (butterfly pea flower extract)
  • Sparkling water
  • Ice cubes

What Makes It Special: The butterfly pea flowers create a natural pH-reactive color that shifts from blue to purple when lime is added, creating an interactive drinking experience.

Passion Mojito

Origin: Inspired by tropical passion fruit cultivations and traditional Caribbean mojito crafting.

Description: A tropical paradise in a glass, combining the exotic sweetness of passion fruit with refreshing mint and lime.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh passion fruit pulp
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Lime juice and zest
  • Sparkling water
  • Ice cubes
  • Natural cane sugar

What Makes It Special: We use fresh passion fruit sourced from sustainable farms, hand-muddling the mint to release essential oils that create an aromatic and flavorful experience.

Strawberry Smoothie

Origin: Modern health-conscious adaptation of traditional fruit beverages, popularized in California health food culture.

Description: A creamy, nutritious blend of fresh strawberries and pure ingredients. Natural sweetness with a smooth, satisfying texture.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh strawberries
  • Pure cow's milk or yogurt
  • Natural honey
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: banana for extra creaminess

What Makes It Special: We use only the ripest strawberries and blend them with our signature pure cow's milk to create a naturally sweet, vitamin-rich drink with no artificial additives.

Watermelon Smoothie

Origin: Inspired by Middle Eastern and Mediterranean traditions of consuming fresh watermelon juice.

Description: A light, refreshing smoothie made with fresh watermelon and ice. Perfect for hot days with its clean, sweet flavor and beautiful pink color.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh watermelon chunks (seedless)
  • Lime juice
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: coconut water for extra hydration

What Makes It Special: We select the sweetest, most flavorful watermelons and blend them fresh to order, preserving maximum nutritional value and natural taste.

Pistachio Shake

Origin: Inspired by Middle Eastern and Mediterranean pistachio dessert traditions.

Description:Rich, creamy, and luxuriously nutty. A sophisticated shake that combines the unique flavor of pistachios with smooth, creamy texture.

Ingredients:

  • Premium pistachios (shelled and roasted)
  • Pure cow's milk
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Natural honey
  • Crushed ice
  • Whipped cream garnish

What Makes It Special: We roast our own pistachios to enhance their natural oils and flavor, then blend them with our pure cow's milk and premium ice cream for an authentically rich taste.

Matcha

Origin: Traditional Japanese tea ceremony culture, where matcha has been revered for over 800 years.

Description: Ceremonial-grade green tea powder whisked to perfection. Earthy, slightly sweet, and packed with antioxidants. Available hot or iced.

Ingredients:

  • Premium ceremonial-grade matcha powder
  • Pure cow's milk (or alternative milk options)
  • Natural sweetener (honey or agave) - optional
  • Hot water (for hot matcha) or ice cubes (for iced matcha)

What Makes It Special: We source authentic matcha from traditional Japanese tea gardens and whisk it using traditional bamboo tools to achieve the perfect frothy consistency and vibrant green color.
